The Prism Approach

Like light through a prism, every Torah teaching reveals multiple dimensions of wisdom. We explore each insight through four distinct lenses:

Pshat

The straightforward meaning - understanding the text in its clear, contextual sense. The foundation upon which all deeper insights rest.

Remez

The hinted meaning - subtle allusions and deeper implications woven into the text. What the Torah suggests beyond its literal words.

Drash

The expounded meaning - insights drawn through creative interpretation and connection to other sources. Where wisdom comes alive.

Sod

The mystical meaning - the deepest spiritual insights and kabbalistic understanding. The soul of Torah wisdom revealed.

Meet Rabbi Asaf

What started as informal Torah sharing over "sushi and scotch" with childhood friends in Toronto has evolved into a global movement of Torah wisdom. Rabbi Asaf's unique journey spans prestigious yeshivas from Toronto to Bnei Brak, where he developed his signature "Prism" approach to Torah learning.

Today, from his home in Jerusalem, Rabbi Asaf continues his daily learning in Kollel while teaching diverse students and sharing weekly insights that reach souls across six continents. His gift lies in making the most profound spiritual concepts feel both accessible and immediately relevant to modern life.
